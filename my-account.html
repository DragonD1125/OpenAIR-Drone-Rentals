Security Deposit Information

A refundable safety deposit is required for all drone rentals. The deposit amounts are as follows:

  • OpenAIR AIR 3: ₹70,000
  • OpenAIR MAVIC 3 PRO: ₹20,000
  • OpenAIR MINI 4 PRO: ₹1,40,000
  • OpenAIR SkyQuest: ₹8,000
  • OpenAIR JoyFlyer: ₹4,000
  • OpenAIR BreezePro: ₹2,000

The security deposit is refundable upon inspection of the drone upon return. If the drone is in undamaged working condition, the full amount will be refunded. Otherwise, part or the entire deposit may be penalized based on the extent of the damage.
